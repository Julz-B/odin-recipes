Brocolli Salad

Description

A crunchy combination of Broccoli, walnuts, raisins & feta, drizzled with a mixture of honey, olive oil & mustard.

  • Broccoli
  • Walnuts
  • Raisins
  • Sunflower seeds
  • Feta
  • Honey
  • Mustard
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Chop the broccoli - add to bowl.
  2. Chop the walnuts - add to bowl.
  3. Add the raisins to the bowl.
  4. Add the sunflower seeds to the bowl.
  5. Mix the honey, mustard and olive oil and mix into the ingredients in the bowl.
  6. Top wih feta.
